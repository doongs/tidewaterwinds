Diversity and COVID-19 Message

All of us at the Tidewater Winds including board members, staff, musicians, conductor, and friends must work together to ensure that racism has no place in the Tidewater Winds or in our local community. We condemn racism, prejudice, discrimination, and oppression. The Tidewater Winds are committed to addressing bias and having courageous conversations that will work to actively dismantle racist and inequitable practices and hold each other accountable. We want to ensure that the Tidewater Winds is inclusive and accessible to all. We invite our entire community to join us in celebrating our diversity, embracing inclusion, and ensuring equity for all. We stand in solidarity with our African-American, Hispanic, and other minority communities. Music and the Tidewater Winds belong to everyone.

As Virginia continues to battle the impact of the Coronavirus, the Tidewater Winds works to maintain a safe environment for work and entertainment. We will continue our safety precautions with small ensembles performing for a mostly online audience. All musicians receive masks, sanitizers, gloves and cleaning materials while working at a safe distance between musicians and others. We continue to follow all guidelines and regulations as presented by our government and community.

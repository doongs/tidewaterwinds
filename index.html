Welcome to the Tidewater Winds!

Tidewater Winds Board and Staff members are comprised of professional musicians, artists, academics, medical professionals, financial leaders, business owners and community leaders.

What brings us together is the love of music and specifically, the love of Tidewater Winds professional concert band.

Our passion for this organization is strong and we are fully committed to enhancing the quality of life for our community through music.

Under the direction of Maestro John Brewington, we are fully engaged in the mission of providing free concerts to the public; as envisioned by our founding director, Sidney Berg.

Our Board and staff work hard to make sure that this Hampton Roads treasure continues to provide free concerts for future generations!

Please join us in preserving this unique American art form and consider a meaningful donation today! Thank you!

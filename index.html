About Us

Tidewater Winds is one of a few professional, non-profit concert bands performing repertoire ranging from Sousa Marches, contemporary repertoire to Big Band programming. When you experience these concerts you are entertained with the highest quality of musical celebration available. Our professional musicians, staff and volunteers are prepared to ensure you are fully engaged and comfortable. Under the direction fo Maestro John Brewington, we provide free concerts every summer and welcome your participation as volunteers, supporters, donors and audience members. Join us for our upcoming concerts, special events and fundraisers. We hope you will support this treasured art form and help us maintain the joy of free summer concerts for all to enjoy.

Selected high school students also perform with the band as part of our Student Intern Program. Scholarship funds are available to selected students who pursue a music degree in college.

Our Mission Statement

The mission of Tidewater Winds is to foster and maintain an organization dedicated to the making of music in the Sousa tradition. Furthermore, it is our charge to maintain the highest aspirations of musical art, creating performances, and providing educational training programs for students at the highest level of excellence.

    Our Goals

  • To serve the community with live performances promoting the appreciation of musical artistry with a unique blend of concert band instrumentation.
  • To enrich, inspire, and create uplifting joy and patriotism in the lives of our audiences with fresh interpretations of a variety of musical compositions.
  • To bring quality entertainment to the community while maintaining integrity to sustain a working community of local artists
  • To provide opportunities to educate and mold young musicians that provide and contribute to artistic growth.

Education

Outreach Projects

We offer a vibrant Student Intern Program along with Master Classes, In-School Clinics, and Informational Music Presentations. At Tidewater Winds, education is more than just having students attend a concert. For us, we involve students backstage and, more importantly, on-stage. We partner with area public schools to find students interested in arts management, arts production, and as musicians to perform with Tidewater Winds. We are the only professional performing ensemble to include students as regular performing members of the band.

We help provide real world experiences to dozens of students annually. Make a difference in the quality of life for those who not only attend our concerts, but for those students who benefit from the expertise of our concert band. Please consider supporting this amazing program!

Adopt an Intern

You provide powerful support to the Tidewater Winds Education Initiative by underwriting a student intern experience with the Winds. Your donation offers mentor opportunities with our professional musicians, Master Class sessions, performance support and a powerful experience for our young students. Additionally, Tidewater Winds provides selected students who pursue music as a degree, scholarship funds for college. This past year, 20 high-school and college interns were chosen to enhance our summer concert series and we thank you for your support!

Our Services

Open Rehearsals

School groups, after school groups, community groups, businesses, and senior living facilities can RSVP to experience one of our rehearsals. While we ask you to remain quiet so the musicians can work, your presence is welcome and a staff member will assist you with our work.

Master Classes

For a nominal fee, you can secure one of our musicians to present a Master Class for your school, studio or special event. Musicians will offer support, direction and guidance in repertoire, technique and rehearsal processes. We would love to help you enhance your musical talents.

Full Concert Band for Hire

Tidewater Winds is a Professional Full-Size Concert Band. With Maestro Brewington conducting, the Winds are available for hire by Agencies, Performers, Music Industry Professionals and other special events. Contact us for pricing and details at 757-480-0953

Little Big Band for Hire

Tidewater Winds 10 to 12 piece Jazz Band is available for special events, concerts, festivals and promotions. Contact our management office at 757-480-0953 for pricing and details.

Small Ensembles For Hire

Tidewater Winds musicians are professional performers available for additional programs, concerts and events as needed. Hire one of our trios, quartets, or other configurations as needed for your events at 757-480-0953

Public Speaking Engagements

The professional staff members of Tidewater Winds are available to speak to your Civic League, Service Group, or Community Program on the work of Tidewater Winds, the need for music in schools, free community concerts, or any other topic appropriate for your needs.

Student Band Clinics

School bands, after school ensembles, community ensembles, can secure members of our Concert Band or our Maestro to serve as a clinician for your bands development. With professional expertise, our members will empower your ensemble to reach their full potential.

Musician-In-Residence

For a nominal fee, you can secure one of our musicians to be present in your school, community. for your event, or just to enhance your project with quality musicianship.

A Message from the Winds

All of us at the Tidewater Winds including board members, staff, musicians, conductor, and friends must work together to ensure that racism has no place in the Tidewater Winds or in our local community. We condemn racism, prejudice, discrimination, and oppression. The Tidewater Winds are committed to addressing bias and having courageous conversations that will work to actively dismantle racist and inequitable practices and hold each other accountable. We want to ensure that the Tidewater Winds is inclusive and accessible to all. We invite our entire community to join us in celebrating our diversity, embracing inclusion, and ensuring equity for all. We stand in solidarity with our African-American, Hispanic, and other minority communities. Music and the Tidewater Winds belong to everyone.

As Virginia continues to battle the impact of the Coronavirus, the Tidewater Winds works to maintain a safe environment for work and entertainment. We will continue our safety precautions with small ensembles performing for a mostly online audience. All musicians receive masks, sanitizers, gloves and cleaning materials while working at a safe distance between musicians and others. We continue to follow all guidelines and regulations as presented by our government and community.

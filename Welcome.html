Tidewater Winds is one of a few professional, non-profit concert bands performing repertoire ranging from Sousa Marches, contemporary repertoire to Big Band programming. When you experience these concerts you are entertained with the highest quality of musical celebration available. Our professional musicians, staff and volunteers are prepared to ensure you are fully engaged and comfortable. Under the direction fo Maestro John Brewington, we provide free concerts every summer and welcome your participation as volunteers, supporters, donors and audience members. Join us for our upcoming concerts, special events and fundraisers. We hope you will support this treasured art form and help us maintain the joy of free summer concerts for all to enjoy.

Who are the Winds?
​
Tidewater Winds Board and Staff members are comprised of professional musicians, artists, academics, medical professionals, financial leaders, business owners and community leaders. What brings us together is the love of music and specifically, the love of Tidewater Winds professional concert band. Our passion for this organization is strong and we are fully committed to enhancing the quality of life for our community through music. We are fully engaged in the mission of providing free concerts to the public; as envisioned by our founding director, Sidney Berg. Our Board and staff work hard to make sure that this Hampton Roads treasure continues to provide free concerts for future generations!  

Please join us in preserving this unique American art form and consider a meaningful donation today! Thank you!
